Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 8, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Trading Simulator. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is processed when you use our game and how it is used. The core game runs entirely in your browser and does not require account registration.

Information We Collect

We do not operate a backend database for player accounts. Core game-related data is stored locally on your device via your browser's Local Storage.

This local data includes:

  • Your account balance and equity.
  • Your trading history and open positions.
  • Your unlocked achievements and game progress.

In addition, when you interact with third‑party services used on this site, those services may process limited technical data (such as IP address, device identifiers, cookies) in accordance with their own privacy policies. If you submit our feedback form, the information you enter (name, email, message) will be transmitted to our email provider to deliver your message to us.

How We Use Information

  • Operate the game and save your progress locally on your device.
  • Display advertising through Google AdSense to support the service.
  • Measure aggregate usage and improve the product using Google Analytics.
  • Respond to your feedback submissions when you choose to contact us.

Cookies and Local Storage

We use your browser's Local Storage to save your game state. This is essential for the game to function correctly.

Third‑party services may set cookies or use similar technologies:

  • Google AdSense: to serve ads, prevent fraud, and cap frequency.
  • Google Analytics: to generate aggregated usage statistics.

You can manage ad personalization at Google Ads Settings, or opt out of interest‑based ads via AboutAds and NAI. For analytics, you may install the Google Analytics Opt‑out Add‑on.

Third‑Party Services

  • Google AdSense (Advertising): We display ads provided by Google. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and/or other sites. Learn more at Google Advertising Policies.
  • Google Analytics (Analytics): We use Google Analytics to understand aggregate usage (e.g., page views, sessions). Data is processed by Google per its Privacy Policy.
  • EmailJS (Contact/Feedback): When you submit the feedback form, your provided name, email (if any), and message are transmitted to EmailJS to deliver your email to us. See EmailJS Privacy Policy.

Data Security

Game progress is stored locally on your device. For data processed by third‑party services, security is governed by those providers. We recommend using a modern, up‑to‑date browser and reviewing the linked privacy policies.

Your Choices

  • Clear local game data anytime via your browser's site data settings.
  • Manage or opt out of personalized ads using the links above.
  • Submit feedback without providing an email (remain anonymous), or request deletion of prior messages.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information via the feedback form, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Please review it periodically.
